meet nadine

I'm Nadine Joy Douds, the creator of NJD Bakery (Getting clarity on the site name now, aye?), a not real company that consists of me spending glorious hours puttering away in the kitchen prior to bombarding friends, family, and coworkers with treats and then eliciting from them the requisite detailed feedback. All the recipes and images on the site are my very own - some turning out splendidly on the first attempt and others taking quite a large amount of finagling to get them right. The goal is to have a plethora of tasty treats and baked goods to meet different dietary and allergy needs, all made from nutrient dense and better-for-you ingredients. Is this easy? Not at all! But, figuring out healthy recipes that don't taste like dust, and sharing those treats and having folks genuinely enjoy them, is one of my most favorite things to do.
