chocolate hazelnut truffles

If you are a fan of the popular hazelnut spreads, you will likely enjoy this pick-up-able, chewable version. These bite sized truffles have a combo of sweetened hazelnut butter blended with cacao and tiny bits of tasty toasted hazelnuts in the middle, surrounded by a larger hazelnut crunch on the outsideâ€¦ that is finished with a drizzle of allergen free chocolate. Heck yeah.

They look like a million bucks, taste like a million bucks, and lucky for you (and not so lucky for me) do not cost a million bucks nor have a million scary ingredients in them. (The second part is good for me too.)

They are the best right out of the fridge or freezer. If you're going more for the hazelnut spread approach, leave them out on the counter for a few hours and you'll soon have quite the mess to lick up. The point is: they melt!
