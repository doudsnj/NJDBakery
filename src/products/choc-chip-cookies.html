chocolate chip cookies

Known as 'Gateway Cookies', these bad boys have fooled many a gluten loving, sugar loving tastebud! They are a staple cookie to keep on hand, great for serving to guests who you want to genuinely enjoy your dessert, and genuinely have no idea it's made from healthier ingredients than the standard SAD cookie. Nothing wrong with a bit of a con ;) (...in this context).

The mix of slight bitterness from the dark chocolate, coupled with the sweetness of caramelized coconut sugar makes these part chewy/part gooey cookies a mouth watering treat that is hard to resist and...full of nutrient dense ingredients. Try a batch and you'll be hooked!
