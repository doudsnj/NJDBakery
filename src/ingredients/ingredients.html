ingredients

Here at NJD Bakery we only use the best quality ingredients, organic whenever possible, free from preservatives, chemicals, dyes, etc. For people with food sensitivities or dietary restrictions, being able to vet out ingredients is a must. We strive for full transparency, so please take a look below and feel free to contact us with any questions.

organic almond flour either homemade almond flour from soaked and dehydrated organic almonds, or the blanched organic almond flour from Nuts.com.
organic almond butter homemade almond butter from soaked and dehydrated organic almonds, or on rare occasion Kirkland's Organic Creamy Almond Butter.
organic eggs When possible, organic eggs are purchased from local farmers. Kirkland's Organic Eggs are also used at times.
