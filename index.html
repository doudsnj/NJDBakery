Everyone should be able to eat food, made from high quality and nutritious ingredients, that tastes good and makes them happy, regardless of dietary restrictions.
At NJD Bakery, our goal is to help make that easier! Choose what you would like from an array of allergen friendly products and we will make those for you.
If you see a product you'd like to have, but its default has ingredients you can't eat, choose an adaptation of that product instead. Eyeballing those chocolate chip cookies but wish they were vegan? Just choose the vegan option when you order! Our aim is to help you and your loved ones eat healthy, tasty food that leaves you satisfied and feeling good. Everyone deserves happy tastebuds.
